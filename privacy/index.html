Numics RN

Privacy Policy

Effective: May 22, 2026

Numics RN ("the App") is an AI-powered nursing education app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique user identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address. Apple may provide a private relay email address on your behalf.

Usage Data. We collect information about how you interact with the App, including tutoring sessions started, practice questions answered, performance scores, and feature usage. This data is used to track your progress and improve the App. We may use anonymized, aggregated performance data across all users to improve question quality, calibrate difficulty levels, and enhance the educational content within the App.

Tutoring Conversations. Messages you send to Kai, the AI tutor, are transmitted to our AI provider to generate responses. We may store conversation history to provide continuity across sessions and to improve the tutoring experience.

Subscription Data. We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat receives your anonymous App Store transaction data to verify your subscription status. We do not receive or store your payment method details — all payment processing is handled by Apple.

Device and Technical Data. We may collect device type, operating system version, app version, and crash logs to diagnose issues and improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize the AI tutoring experience
  • Track your progress across NCLEX-RN content categories
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Diagnose bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • Improve the App and develop new features

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Supabase — backend database and authentication. Stores your account information, progress data, and tutoring history.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — powers Kai, the AI tutor. Your tutoring messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Anthropic's usage policies apply to this processing.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Receives anonymized transaction data from Apple to verify your subscription status.
  • Apple (Sign in with Apple) — authentication. We receive only the information you authorize Apple to share.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and progress data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or to resolve disputes.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for nursing students and is not directed at children under 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 17. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Account Deletion. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Email. If you shared your email address, you can contact us to update or remove it.
  • Subscription. You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID Account Settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or by other means. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
